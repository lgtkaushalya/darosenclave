Traditional Children’s Play Items

Eco‑friendly toys made from nature—simple, creative, and timeless.

Overview

← Back to Activities

Introduce your children to traditional Sri Lankan toys like Gokkola Nalawa (palm‑leaf kites) and Kurumbatti (coconut‑shell spinning tops). These handmade items encourage creativity and imagination, offering an authentic break from digital screens. Parents often join in, rediscovering childhood fun alongside their kids. This is one of the best family‑friendly activities near Mirissa for guests who want cultural learning mixed with play.

Highlights & Tips

  • Hands‑on crafting and play
  • Made from palm, coconut, and natural fibers
  • Great for families with young children
  • Photo‑friendly, culturally rich
