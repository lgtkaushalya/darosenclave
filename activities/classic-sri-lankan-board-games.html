Classic Sri Lankan Board Games

Social, strategic, and joyfully competitive—bring everyone to the table.

Overview

← Back to Activities

Bring the family together over classic board games like Pancha and Puwakkola Patha. These traditional Sri Lankan board games are social, strategic, and often hilarious, sparking laughter late into the evening. They’re a great way to relax after a day outdoors and to experience how locals entertained themselves for generations.

Highlights & Tips

  • Great for groups and families
  • Learn simple rules quickly
  • Indoor fun for rainy days
  • Connect with local culture
